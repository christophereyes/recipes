Ingredients: 2 pieces of bread, jelly of choice, (crunchy) peanut butter.

Step 1: Sit both pieces of bread in toaster for about a minute.
Step 2: Once toasted spread a thick layer of peanut butter one one end
and a less heavy layer of jelly.
Step 3: Close the two pieces of bread together and cut at an angle.
Step 4: Enjoy!
